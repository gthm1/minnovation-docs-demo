# AlphaX Cloud Overview

AlphaX Cloud is Minnovation's platform for managing connected IoT/OT devices at scale.
This guide covers the basics of signing in, adding a site, and viewing live device data.

## Signing in

1. Navigate to `cloud.alphax.example` and sign in with your organisation credentials.
2. If this is your first login, you'll be prompted to accept the workspace invite sent
   by your administrator.

## Adding your first site

1. From the dashboard, select **Sites → Add Site**.
2. Enter the site name and address, then choose a device profile.
3. Once saved, the site appears on your map view with a status indicator.

## Viewing device data

Each device tile shows live telemetry, last-seen time, and connection health. Click a
tile to open the detailed view, including historical trends and alert history.
